SynchroTrack

my first real UX project (and yes, it shows)

Remember when every productivity app made you feel MORE stressed? I built SynchroTrack because I needed something that wouldn't judge me for needing a "calm focus" mode at 3pm. It's basically Spotify moods but for your brain.

  • Three modes because decision fatigue is real
  • No notifications, timers, or guilt trips
  • Gradients that don't hurt your eyes at 2am
  • My first time using Figma components (and it worked!)
